We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or exact birth details through analytics.

The App uses analytics tools (such as PostHog) to understand feature usage and improve the app experience. Analytics data is collected in a non-identifiable and aggregated way.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data Usage

Collected data is used only to:

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers. Data is not used for cross-app tracking.
