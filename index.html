Privacy Policy

Khmer Calendar: Smart Reminder

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to our design. Khmer Calendar: Smart Reminder ("the App") is built to ensure that your personal data remains private, local, and under your control.

Privacy at a Glance

Data Storage 100% Local. Profile and reminders never leave your device.
Tracking Anonymous usage analytics via PostHog. No personal info tracked.
Third Parties Google AdMob (Ads) and Apple/Google (Payments).
Data Sale We never sell or rent your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information (Local Only)

The following information is stored using AsyncStorage exclusively on your device. We have no technical means to access this data:

  • Profile Data: Name, date, time, and place of birth.
  • Preferences: Language, theme, and font settings.
  • User Content: Reminders, notes, and partner birth data for compatibility features.

Usage Analytics (Non-Identifiable)

We use PostHog to collect aggregated data to improve app performance. This includes interaction events like tab switches or subscription restores. This data is stripped of any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

To provide a free version of the app, we use AdMob. They may use device identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt-out via your OS settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads

In-App Purchases

Transactions are handled securely by Apple and Google. We receive a "receipt" to unlock your features, but we never see your credit card or billing details.

3. Data Security & Deletion

Because your data is local, its security depends on your device's security (Passcode, FaceID, etc.).

  • Uninstallation: Deleting the app removes all local data permanently.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not back up your profile to our servers, ensuring you have total ownership.
